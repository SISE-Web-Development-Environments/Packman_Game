This game created by yzhar chernov and dean blinder. the plugin we used was the datepicker, the templete we used was the register and login pages. in the beginning we did not know how detect our errors and we spend a lot of time not knowing what was wrong.


the rules of the game:
eat as much ballls as you can,
small balls are 5 points,
medium balls are 15 points,
large balls are 25 points.
if you eat the clock you gain time.
if you eat the coronaVirus you gain one life.
you have 5 life if ghost eats you, you lose one life.
good luck.
